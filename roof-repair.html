Roof Repair at Its Finest

The life of our roofs greatly depends on how we take care of it. But the effort to do this is often beyond the abilities of the regular Joe. This is where Rick's Roofing & Construction comes in. We do not claim to be the leader in residential roof in Collinsville, TX. But we can guarantee that your roof is in great hands. So if you want to learn more about how we can help you, then you should pay close attention to what we say.

If a customer asks us to do roof repair, our first task is to carefully inspect the visible roof. Most of the time if the damage is already conspicuous, it would often mean that there is a need for us to completely replace the roof. The more visible the damage is, the more serious is the problem. We would also inspect the attic if you have one because this particular room bears the most number of evidence of roof damage. We would also check if there are signs of mold infestation.

Our next step in this roof repair project is to determine the cause of the roof damage. If the damage is brought about by the weather, fixing it while it is still small will prevent things from going from being bad to worse. We also need to ensure that the support of your roof can still carry its weight. This is necessary if we have determined that the root cause of the problem is the flashing. Considering that the primary function of the flashing is to direct water away from the house so if it is no longer working, the initial fatality would be your roof.
